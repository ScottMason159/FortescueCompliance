Statutory Positions App

A simple, step-by-step guide for making and managing statutory appointments.

!
Before you start

The request should be completed by the appointee or their direct leader. Contractors must be mobilised to site.

Have these ready

  • Your SuccessFactors email and password
  • The correct site, department and position
  • Current certificates loaded in SuccessFactors
  • Time to read and accept each declaration

Main process

Make a statutory appointment

Complete these steps in order. Do not book the SSE sign-off session until the earlier steps are complete.

Step 1 of 7

Step 1

Sign in with your SuccessFactors details

  1. Open the Statutory Positions App from the Fortescue Hub App Centre.
  2. Contractors use the link in the Contractor Extranet Hub.
  3. Enter the same email and password you use for SuccessFactors.
Cannot sign in? Check that you are mobilised to site. For technical issues, contact the IT Service Desk.

Step 2

Start a new request

  1. Select Make An Appointment.
  2. Select Statutory Appointment Request.
Only continue if: you are the appointee or the appointee's direct leader.

Step 3

Select the correct statutory position

  1. Review the available position types.
  2. Select the position that matches the person's actual duties.
  3. Common choices include Electrical Supervisor and Statutory Supervisor.
Not sure? Stop and confirm the correct position with site leadership or HS Compliance before continuing.

Step 4

Enter the appointee details

  1. Search for the appointee by name or FID.
  2. Confirm the site, department, work group and operational area.
  3. Read each declaration carefully.
  4. Choose whether this request replaces an existing appointment.
Check before submitting: The person, site and role must all be correct.

Step 5

Check competency evidence

  1. Read the evidence requirements shown in the app.
  2. Make sure certificates are loaded in SuccessFactors.
  3. Send external Statements of Attainment or exam certificates to the site Training team.
  4. Complete the disclaimer and submit.
The request will stop if a required certificate is missing from SuccessFactors.
Do not send competency evidence to HS Compliance. Use the site Training team.

Step 6

Complete the SSE and appointee sign-off

  1. Wait for instructions in SuccessFactors.
  2. Attend the sign-off session with the site's GM, who is the SSE.
  3. The SSE signs the request in the app.
  4. The appointee then opens the request and signs their acceptance.
Do not book or attend the sign-off session until all earlier steps are complete.

Step 7

Check the appointment is active

  1. Authoriser: waiting for the SSE to sign.
  2. Pending Acceptance: waiting for the appointee to sign.
  3. Active: the appointment is complete. No further action is required.
Finished: The status must show Active.

HSR process

Add or manage a Health and Safety Representative

The HSR process is shorter and does not have an approval workflow.

1

Open the HSR area

Select Make An Appointment, then HSR Appointment Request.

2

Complete the details

Enter the HSR, person who conducted the election, site, department, crew and election date.

3

Submit and check the register

The HSR becomes active immediately. There is no approval step.

Expiry reminder: Emails are sent three months before the three-year election period ends.

Special process

Make an SSE or Alternate SSE appointment

This process is different because the appointee accepts first, then the Mine Operator authorises the appointment.

1

Select Site Senior Executive.

2

Enter details and complete competency requirements.

3

Complete the tailings acknowledgement where the site has a TSF.

4

The appointee signs acceptance.

5

Contact HS Compliance to arrange Mine Operator sign-off.

End an appointment

Rescind an appointment

Use this when an appointment is no longer needed or valid.

  1. Open the appointment record.
  2. Select Rescind Appointment in the top-right corner.
  3. Enter a clear reason.
  4. Confirm the appointment now shows as rescinded.
The reason becomes part of the appointment record. Keep it factual and clear.

Need help?

Cannot access the app

Confirm you are mobilised to site and are using your SuccessFactors account. Contact the IT Service Desk for technical issues.

Certificate is missing

Send the certificate to your site Training team so it can be loaded into SuccessFactors.

Unsure about the appointment

Contact HS Compliance or site leadership before submitting the request.
